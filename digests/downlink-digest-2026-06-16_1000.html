01 Actively Exploited Fortinet and cPanel Vulnerabilities

CISA added an actively exploited LiteSpeed cPanel user-end plugin vulnerability, identified in the reporting as CVE-2026-48172, to its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities Catalog. The flaw affects LiteSpeed cPanel user-end plugin versions before 2.4.8 and reportedly allows attackers with FTP or web shell access to escalate privileges to root on shared hosting servers running CloudLinux/CageFS. CISA ordered U.S. Federal Civilian Executive Branch agencies to secure affected systems within three days under BOD 26-04 guidance.

Fortinet FortiSandbox also appeared in exploitation reporting, with Defused Cyber observing activity against CVE-2026-39813, CVE-2026-39808, and CVE-2026-25089. 02 Malware, Ransomware, and Abuse of Trusted Channels

Zimperium documented Rokarolla, a new Android banking trojan targeting 217 banking and cryptocurrency apps with support for 137 remote commands. The malware is distributed through malicious sites impersonating popular applications such as TikTok and Chrome, with an initial dropper posing as Google Play Protect. Once installed, it abuses Accessibility permissions to disable protections, capture lock-screen credentials, deploy fake login overlays, read and send SMS messages, log screens and keystrokes, and silently replace copied cryptocurrency wallet addresses.

DragonForce ransomware was reported abusing Microsoft Teams TURN relay infrastructure through a custom Go-based backdoor named Backdoor.Turn. Symantec described it as the first known in-the-wild malware to use Teams relay servers to mask command-and-control traffic. In a December 2025 intrusion against a major U.S. services company, the attackers likely gained access through an unknown SQL or MSSQL server flaw, used BYOVD techniques with multiple vulnerable drivers to obtain kernel-level privileges, exfiltrated data, and deployed ransomware.

Business email compromise reporting from IRONSCALES showed a same-day payment request sent from a legitimate construction consulting domain via hosted Exchange infrastructure.
